Secondary Course

In secondary school, young minds start to explore their options career-wise. This education stage follows a curriculum that helps students find their passions and abilities, which they can use in their future professional development.

This Course is equivalent to the Xth standard. You can choose subjects from the Scheme of Studies given in Table-. However, you will be required to complete a minimum of six subjects with at least one language or at most two languages, which is compulsory for certification.

We advise the learners to select the subjects as per their plan of further career opportunities.
