ACCOUNTING BRANCH

Our Accounting Branch deals expeditiously with matters related to finance of the Board-
1. They maintain detailed descriptions of Receipt and Payment of the Board.
2. They maintain records of remuneration paid to the entire staff, including Zonal Supervising Officer, Supervising Officer and Examiners.
3. They monitor and audit the payment towards Employee's Pension, GPF, TA, DA etc.
4. They prepare an annual budget.
