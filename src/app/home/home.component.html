Building The Future That Holds Great Opportunities

Education is the bend point for every living being to transcend their limitations. It is a well-known fact that many children in India are not getting the proper education. There are various reasons for this disparity in education between the privileged and the underprivileged. To bridge this gap, great charitable organizations, NGOs, boards such as NBSE are making it possible for the gifted and hardworking young minds to get great educational opportunities that they never had before.

Since its inception in 2019, NBSE has become the torchbearer of giving good education opportunities with the 'Education For All' motto. .. We aim to achieve the welfare of every child through proper education as currently, there are more than 57 million children who do not know what it's like to step inside a classroom as they don't have access to the schools. NBSE has already taken steps to pave the way for education by ensuring free education to brilliant young minds by providing them with books, all kinds of learning resources, and access to schools nearby to their homes.

The whole purpose is to unite the entire country through education by giving a platform to the underprivileged, weaker section who may have fewer resources but have the spirit to achieve success in life. Read more
